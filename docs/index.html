Features

Everything you need to lock your apps.

More features than paid alternatives — and it's completely free.

🤚

Touch ID

Unlock protected apps instantly with your fingerprint. Single prompt — no double authentication like other apps.

⌚

Apple Watch Unlock

Automatically unlock apps when your Apple Watch is on your wrist and nearby. Uses BLE wrist detection — no other app locker does this.

📴

Auto-Close on Sleep

Messaging apps like WhatsApp and iMessage are terminated when your laptop sleeps. No one can snoop your notifications.

⏱

Auto-Close Inactive Apps

Protected apps are automatically closed after a configurable inactivity timeout. Per-app control — choose which apps get auto-closed.

🖥

Multi-Monitor Overlay

Full-screen blur overlay appears on all connected displays simultaneously. No way to peek at content on another monitor.

🚨

Panic Key

Emergency exit with Cmd+Opt+Shift+Ctrl+U. System apps like Terminal and Xcode can never be locked. Multiple safety failsafes built in.

How It Works

Three steps to protect your apps.

Set it up once, MakLock runs silently in your menu bar.

Add your apps

Open MakLock settings and add the apps you want to protect. Toggle per-app settings like auto-close.

Switch to a protected app

When you (or anyone) opens or switches to a protected app, MakLock instantly shows a blur overlay blocking all content.

Authenticate

Touch ID, Apple Watch proximity, or password. Once authenticated, the app is yours. It re-locks on idle, sleep, or when your Watch leaves range.
